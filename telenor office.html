KS & Associates feels honor to provide consultancy services to Telenor Pakistan. Project description


Telenor Regional head Quarter at Shahra e Faisal Karachi isthe major & most recent project. The project involves interior designing extending up to six office floors & 3 Parking floors. The project focuses on providing Auditorium, Board rooms, Conference Rooms 20 no, Meeting Rooms 12 no. Lounges, Working Halls for 400 persons, Cafeteria & commercial Kitchen etc.

Approach to Architecture


To incorporate both Modern &Contemporary themes were adopted to deliver luxurious and spacious office environment. Modern Design Elements & Furniture items were incorporated to achieve the requirements.
